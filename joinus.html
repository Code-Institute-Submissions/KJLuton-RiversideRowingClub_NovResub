Riverside Rowing Club

is a rowing club in Putney, based on the Tideway of River Thames.

The club was founded in 1990. We are the youngest club on the Tideway and very competitive in all categories. We have an active Men’s and Women’s senior squad as well as a large Master’s Squad, who also compete throughout the year. We also run a ‘Learn to Row’ course every summer for people who are new to the sport.

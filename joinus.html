Riverside Rowing Club

is a rowing club based in Putney, on the River Thames.

The club was founded in 1987. We are the youngest club on the Tideway and very competitive in all categories. We have an active Men’s and Women’s senior squad as well as a large Master’s Squad, who also compete throughout the year. We also run a ‘Learn to Row’ course every summer for people who are new to the sport.

Squads

Senior Squads

The Senior Squad is aimed at men and women who have previously rowed at School, University or another club. Our senior squad trains 6 days a week with the ultimate aim; to win.

Master's Squad

The Master's Squad is aimed at men and women who have some experience in rowing and are over 30. Our Master's squad is competitve but is also fun! They train around work commitments and usually when the sun is shining.

Coxes

Riverside Rowing Club welcomes new and experienced coxes. Coxes can join whichever suqad they would like!

Novice Squad

Our Novice Squad is aimed at new rowers who have never been in a boat before. This squad may also cater for you if it has been 10+ years since you've rowed. We run 'Learn to Row" courses in the September for anyone looking to start rowing. We recommend taking these courses before you join.

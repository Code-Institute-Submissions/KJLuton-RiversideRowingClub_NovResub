Racing and Training Kit

RRC Racing All in One - £80

The 'Racing AIO' is compulsory for all racing members. You will wear this on race day to represent the club. It is a requirement of British Rowing that all club members wear the same race kit.

RRC Training All in One - £70

The 'Training AIO' is recommended for training sessions so you can support the club on the water when you're not racing. It is not a requirement but a must-have if you're a die-hard club member!

RRC Long Sleeve Top - £50

The Long Sleeve Top can be used for training or racing conditions. We recommend that you get the short sleeve top for winter racing.

RRC Short Sleeve Top - £30

The Short Sleeve Top can be used for training or racing conditions. We recommend that you get the short sleeve top for summer racing.

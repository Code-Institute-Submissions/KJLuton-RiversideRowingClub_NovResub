Welcome to Riverside Rowing Club

About Us

Riverside Rowing Club is a rowing club based on the Embankment in Putney (London), on the River Thames. The club was founded in 1987. We are the youngest club on the Putney Embankment and very competitive in all categories.
We have a men’s and women’s Senior Squad, Junior Squad, Master's Squad and Novice Squad. All Squads have an allocated coach and training programme.
If you are new to rowing, we run a ‘Learn to Row’ course every September.
The club competes at multiple events throughout the year and we always have a big celebration at the end of the season to celebrate the clubs efforts throughout the year.
Some of the main races we participate in are: Pairs Head (October), Head of the River Race (March), Hammersmith Regatta (May), Met Regatta (June), Henley Womens Regatta (June), Henley Royal Regatta (July).
To find the full rowing calendar and dates they are being held on, for the coming season, please visit the British Rowing calendar in our 'Useful Links' section below.
If you are interested in joining the club as an experienced athlete or a total beginner, visit our Join-Us page to find out more about the club and our membership options.

Racing and Training Kit

Our club kit is designed by Termaxx. We have RRC Club Kit for racing and training conditions. All club members are required to purchase club kit if they wish to race. To order your kit, or if you have any queries relating to kit, please contact the Kit Secretary by filling out the contact form and selecting your reason for contact as 'Kit/Merchandise'.


RRC Racing All in One
(£80)

The 'Racing AIO' is compulsory for all racing members. You will wear this on race day to represent the club. It is a requirement of British Rowing that all club members wear the same race kit.

RRC Training Leggings
(£60)

The Training Leggings are recommended for training sessions so you can support the club on the water when you're not racing. It is not a requirement but a must-have if you're a die-hard club member!

RRC Long Sleeve Top
(£50)

The Long Sleeve Top can be used for training or racing conditions. We recommend that you get the long sleeve top for winter racing.




RRC Short Sleeve Top
(£30)

The Short Sleeve Top can be used for training or racing conditions. We recommend that you get the short sleeve top for summer racing.
